# Your website, in plain English

Hi Sean! Your website is **seanhase.ca**. You control everything on it from
**admin.seanhase.ca**. Sign in with your password there. This page explains what each
part does. No tech knowledge needed.

## The five tabs

**Dashboard** shows what needs your attention: upcoming appointments, whether your
calendar link is working, and any problems. There's also a special link you can add
to Google Calendar or your iPhone calendar once, and after that every new booking
shows up in your own calendar automatically.

**Content** is every word and picture on your website. Change anything, hit
"Save draft" (nothing public changes yet), and when you're happy hit
"Publish to site". It takes about a minute to go live. You can also upload a new
photo of yourself here.

One important thing about wording: "RMT" and "Registered Massage Therapist" are
protected titles. Until you're registered, keep the wording about being a student
(the site already does this). The day you register, come here and update it. 🎉

**Availability** is where you control bookings:
- **Weekly hours**: the windows people can book. Split shifts are fine (add two
  windows to the same day).
- **Booking rules**: how long a session is, how much gap you want between
  appointments, how much warning you need before a new booking (minimum notice), and
  the "bumper" (see below).
- **Sync your calendar**: paste your Google/Apple calendar's secret address here and
  the site will never offer times where you already have something going on.
- **Days off**: block whole days (holidays, exams).

**The bumper**: if you have something at 1pm for an hour and your bumper is 15
minutes, the next appointment someone can book starts at 2:15. It protects your
travel and reset time around real-life commitments.

**Bookings** lists everyone who booked, with their contact info and notes. You can
cancel an appointment here; if you do, message the client yourself since the site
doesn't email them (their confirmation screen told them to save a link where they can
see the change).

**Settings**:
- **Notification email**: where new bookings and messages get sent. If you change it,
  you'll get a confirmation email at the new address, click the link in it or
  notifications stop.
- **Password**: change it any time. Forgot it? Use "Forgot password?" on the sign-in
  page and a reset link goes to your notification email.
- Ignore the "Advanced" box unless Shane asks you to touch it.

## What your clients see

They pick a day, pick a time, enter their name and email, and instantly get a
confirmation screen with an "add to calendar" button and a private link to cancel if
they need to. You get an email with their details and a calendar file.

## If something looks wrong

The site emails you automatically if your calendar link breaks or anything else needs
attention. When in doubt, tell Shane what the Dashboard says.
